Biography

Ramón A. Delgado received his professional title of Ingeniero Civil Electrónico and Master degree in Electronic Engineering from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Chile, in 2009. He received a Ph.D degree in electrical engineering from the University of Newcastle, Australia, in 2014, where he is currently a Research Academic. In this role, Dr Delgado is mainly focussed on the collaboration project between the University of Newcastle and the Swedish company Ericsson AB.

His research interests include system identification, control, signal processing and optimisation.
