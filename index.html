Biography

Ramón A. Delgado was born in San Felipe, Chile, in 1984. He received his professional title of Ingeniero Civil Electrónico and Master degree in Electronic Engineering from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Chile, in 2009. He received a Ph.D degree in electrical engineering from the University of Newcastle, Australia, in 2014. He is currently a Research Academic at the University of Newcastle. In his current role, Dr Delgado is mainly focussed on the current collaboration project between the University of Newcastle and the Swedish company Ericsson AB. His research interests include system identification, control and signal processing.
